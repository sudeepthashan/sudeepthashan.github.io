About the owners


Happi Sushi is a sushi bar owned by Micheal Chen and Daniel Martinez, who wish to bring the wonderful world of Asian cuisine to the suburbs of Long Island. We offer an authentic and classical dining experience with high quality sushi and finely made drinks. With a traditional but welcoming atmosphere, we aim to do honor to the Asian cultures while welcoming all guests to introduce them to the fine cuisines of Japan, Thailand, and Vietnam.
